<p><p>Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) splitters are essential components in fiber optic networks, used to distribute optical signals. The market is primarily categorized into 1×N and 2×N PLC splitters. </p><p>1×N PLC splitters have one input and multiple outputs, making them suitable for applications where a single signal needs to be distributed to several endpoints. In contrast, 2×N PLC splitters feature two inputs and multiple outputs, allowing for more complex signal distribution and redundancy, catering to varied network configurations and enhancing system versatility.</p></p>
