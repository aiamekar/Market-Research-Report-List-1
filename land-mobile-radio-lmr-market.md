<p><strong>The Land Mobile Radio LMR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>40MHz – 174MHz (VHF)</li><li>200MHz – 512MHz (UHF)</li><li>700MHz – 1000MHz (SHF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Land Mobile Radio (LMR) market is segmented into three primary frequency ranges. The VHF band (40MHz – 174MHz) is often used for long-distance communication, suitable for rural and wide-area coverage. The UHF band (200MHz – 512MHz) offers shorter wavelengths, ideal for urban environments with obstacles, providing clearer signals. The SHF band (700MHz – 1000MHz) serves specialized applications, including high-data-rate communications and public safety. Each band serves distinct functionalities based on range, environment, and signal quality.</p></p>
