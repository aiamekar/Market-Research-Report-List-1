Scientific Data Management Systems (SDMS) organize and archive complex scientific data. Chromatography Data Systems (CDS) manage chromatographic data and instrument control. Laboratory Execution Systems (LES) facilitate the execution of lab processes. Other emerging solutions may include specialized software for niche laboratory applications or integrated platforms for comprehensive data management.</p></p>
