<p><p>The LCD sealant market is primarily divided into single-component and two-component types. Single-component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture, offering convenience and ease of application for various surfaces. In contrast, two-component sealants require mixing of a resin and hardener before application, providing enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types cater to different applications in electronics, automotive, and construction industries, depending on specific bonding needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
