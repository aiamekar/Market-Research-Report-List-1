Flat Panel Detectors offer high-resolution imaging with reduced radiation exposure, utilizing seamless digital capture for enhanced diagnostics. In contrast, Linear Array Detectors utilize an array of sensors to capture images linearly, providing real-time imaging capabilities ideal for fast-paced environments. Both technologies are critical in advancing medical imaging, improving patient outcomes, and enhancing workflow efficiency in healthcare settings.</p></p>
