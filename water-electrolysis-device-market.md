<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water electrolysis device market features two primary types: Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers and Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzers. Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte, offering cost-effectiveness and durability for large-scale hydrogen production. In contrast, PEM Electrolyzers use a solid polymer membrane, providing higher efficiency, faster response times, and greater operational flexibility, making them suitable for dynamic applications and renewable energy integration. Both technologies are crucial for advancing hydrogen production and supporting clean energy initiatives.</p></p>
