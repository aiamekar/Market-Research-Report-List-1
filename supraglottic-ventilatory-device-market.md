<p><p>The supraglottic ventilatory device market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Laryngeal Ventilator Devices**: Positioned above the vocal cords, these devices provide effective airway management in emergencies and anesthesia.</p><p>2. **Nasopharyngeal Ventilator Devices**: Inserted through the nasal passage, these devices facilitate ventilation without obstructing oral access, ideal for unconscious patients.</p><p>3. **Oropharyngeal Ventilator Devices**: Placed in the mouth, these devices help maintain an open airway and assist in ventilation during resuscitation efforts.</p><p>Each type serves distinct clinical scenarios.</p></p>
