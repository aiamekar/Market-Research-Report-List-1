<p><p>The Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGMs) market is primarily divided into two types: wearable and implantable devices. Wearable CGMs are typically sensor-based devices that attach to the skin, providing real-time glucose monitoring without invasive procedures. They are user-friendly and offer data visibility for individuals with diabetes. Implantable CGMs, on the other hand, involve a small sensor placed under the skin for long-term glucose tracking, requiring minimal maintenance. Both types enhance glucose management, improving patient outcomes and lifestyle flexibility.</p></p>
