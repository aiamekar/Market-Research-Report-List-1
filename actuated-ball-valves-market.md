<p><strong>The Actuated Ball Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrically Actuated Ball Valves</li><li>Pneumatically Actuated Ball Valves</li><li>Air Actuated Ball Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The actuated ball valves market is primarily categorized into three types: electrically actuated ball valves, pneumatically actuated ball valves, and air actuated ball valves. Electrically actuated valves utilize electric motors for precise control and responsiveness, making them ideal for automated systems. Pneumatically actuated valves use compressed air for operation, offering rapid response and high-speed actuation, often in industrial applications. Air actuated valves, similar to pneumatic types, specifically use air pressure, providing reliable performance in various fluid control processes, including HVAC and manufacturing.</p></p>
