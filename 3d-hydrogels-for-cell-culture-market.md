<p><p>The 3D hydrogels for cell culture market can be categorized into two main types: scaffold-free and scaffold-based systems. Scaffold-free hydrogels utilize self-assembling cell clusters to create structures that mimic natural tissue environments, promoting cell-cell interactions. In contrast, scaffold-based hydrogels provide a supportive framework that enables cells to adhere, grow, and differentiate, often enhancing mechanical stability and nutrient diffusion. Both types play crucial roles in tissue engineering and regenerative medicine, catering to diverse research and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
