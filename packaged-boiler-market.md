<p><p>The packaged boiler market primarily consists of two main types: shell boilers and drum boilers. Shell boilers are compact, cylindrical units designed for steam generation, ideal for small to medium applications due to their efficiency and ease of installation. Drum boilers, on the other hand, feature a large drum filled with water and steam, suitable for high-capacity demand settings. Both types serve diverse industries, including manufacturing and energy, by providing reliable steam and hot water solutions tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
