<p><p>The semiconductor polishing pads market is primarily divided into hard and soft Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads. Hard CMP pads are designed for aggressive polishing applications, providing durability and precision for advanced processes, such as flattening silicon wafers. In contrast, soft CMP pads offer better conformability and are used for more delicate polishing tasks, ensuring a smoother finish while reducing defects. Both types are essential for achieving desired surface characteristics in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to different processing requirements.</p></p>
