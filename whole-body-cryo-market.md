<p><p>The Whole Body Cryo market comprises three main types: Liquid Nitrogen Therapy, Dry Ice Therapy, and Electric Cryo Therapy. Liquid Nitrogen Therapy utilizes liquid nitrogen to rapidly cool the body, promoting recovery and reducing inflammation. Dry Ice Therapy employs solid carbon dioxide to achieve similar effects but can be less efficient for full-body treatments. Electric Cryo Therapy uses refrigerant-circulating technology for precise temperature control, providing a more user-friendly and consistent experience, appealing to both athletes and wellness enthusiasts.</p></p>
