<p><strong>The Infrared Contrast Horizon Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Infrared Contrast Horizon Sensor</li><li>Passive Infrared Contrast Horizon Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Contrast Horizon Sensor market is divided into two primary types: active and passive infrared sensors. Active infrared contrast horizon sensors emit their own infrared radiation, detecting variations in thermal contrast to discern horizon lines and obstacles. In contrast, passive infrared contrast horizon sensors rely on ambient infrared radiation, capturing heat signatures from the environment to identify horizon boundaries. Both types are crucial in applications like aviation, robotics, and autonomous vehicles, enhancing navigation and safety by providing reliable horizon detection.</p></p>
