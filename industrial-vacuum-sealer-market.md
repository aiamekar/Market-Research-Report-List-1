<p><ul><li>Nozzle Vacuum Sealer</li><li>Chamber Vacuum Sealer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial vacuum sealer market comprises primarily two types: nozzle vacuum sealers and chamber vacuum sealers. Nozzle vacuum sealers utilize a nozzle to extract air from bags or containers, making them suitable for bulky items and varying package sizes. In contrast, chamber vacuum sealers are designed for sealing products within a sealed chamber, allowing for more consistent sealing and greater efficiency for large quantities. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring product freshness and extending shelf life.</p></p>
