<p><p>The Advanced Suspension Control market encompasses two primary types: semi-active and active suspension systems. Semi-active suspension systems adjust their damping characteristics in response to road conditions and driving dynamics, enhancing ride comfort and stability. In contrast, active suspension systems utilize sensors and actuators to actively control wheel movement and vehicle posture, offering superior handling and ride quality. Both types aim to improve vehicle performance, safety, and comfort while adapting to varying driving scenarios and environments.</p></p>
