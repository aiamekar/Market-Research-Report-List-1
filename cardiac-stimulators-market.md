<p><strong>The Cardiac Stimulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Temporary cardiac stimulator</li><li>Single-chamber cardiac pacing stimulator</li><li>Dual-chamber cardiac pacing stimulator</li><li>Single chamber cardioverter defibrillator</li><li>Dual-chamber cardioverter defibrillator</li><li>Resynchronized pacing</li><li>Three-chamber resynchronization automatic cardioversion defibrillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac stimulators market consists of various types that cater to specific cardiac conditions. Temporary cardiac stimulators are used short-term for urgent situations. Single-chamber cardiac pacing stimulators stimulate one heart chamber, while dual-chamber stimulators target both. Single and dual-chamber cardioverter defibrillators deliver shocks to restore rhythm. Resynchronized pacing improves heart function in heart failure patients, while three-chamber resynchronization automatic cardioversion defibrillators provide advanced therapy by coordinating contractions across all chambers for optimal cardiac performance.</p></p>
