<p><ul><li>Reactive Adhesives</li><li>Non-Reactive Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace adhesives and sealants market is categorized into reactive and non-reactive adhesives. Reactive adhesives undergo a chemical reaction during the curing process, forming strong bonds, making them ideal for high-stress applications such as composite materials and metal assemblies. Non-reactive adhesives, on the other hand, are physically bonded without chemical change and are often used for assembly where flexibility and ease of application are prioritized. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring safety, durability, and performance in aerospace applications.</p></p>
