<p><p>The freestanding smoke alarm market is primarily divided into two types: ceiling-mounted and wall-mounted units. Ceiling-mounted smoke alarms are designed to be installed on ceilings, where smoke rises, providing optimal detection when smoke begins to accumulate. In contrast, wall-mounted smoke alarms are affixed to walls, typically at a height of 12 inches from the ceiling to enhance responsiveness. Both types are essential for home safety, accommodating different building designs and preferences while ensuring effective smoke detection in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
