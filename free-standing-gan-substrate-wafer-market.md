<p><p>The free-standing GaN substrate wafer market is categorized into non-polar and semi-polar types. Non-polar GaN substrates exhibit isotropic properties, minimizing crack formation and achieving high-quality epitaxy, ideal for high-power and high-frequency devices. Semi-polar GaN substrates, on the other hand, reduce piezoelectric fields and enhance luminous efficiency, making them suitable for optoelectronic applications like LEDs and laser diodes. Both types cater to diverse applications, driven by demands for improved performance in electronic and photonic devices.</p></p>
