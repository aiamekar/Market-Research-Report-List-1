<p><p>Cystoid Macular Edema (CME) treatment involves various market segments. Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) reduce inflammation and pain, while Anti-Vascular Endothelial Growth Factor (VEGF) agents target abnormal blood vessel growth. Carbonic Anhydrase Inhibitors work by decreasing fluid production in the eye, alleviating pressure and swelling. Steroids, both topical and systemic, are potent anti-inflammatory agents used to manage CME. Each category plays a crucial role in alleviating symptoms and improving visual outcomes in CME patients.</p></p>
