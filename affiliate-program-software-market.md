<p><p>Affiliate program software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted online, offering flexibility, scalability, and easy accessibility from any device with internet access. They typically operate on a subscription model, reducing upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing more control over data security and customization. However, it often requires significant initial investment and ongoing maintenance, making it less flexible than cloud options.</p></p>
