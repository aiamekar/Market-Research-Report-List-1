<p><p>Roll-to-Roll (R2R) technology encompasses various printing methods tailored for continuous substrate processing. Imprint lithography provides precise patterning, and Flexographic printing is ideal for packaging materials. Laser photo ablation allows for detailed removal of material, while Offset printing delivers vibrant colors on flexible substrates. Inkjet printing provides significant customization and efficiency, making it a popular choice for diverse industries.</p></p>
