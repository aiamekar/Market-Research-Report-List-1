<p><p>An orifice flow meter is a device used to measure the flow rate of fluids through a pipeline. It operates by creating a pressure drop across an orifice plate, which is installed within the pipeline. The differential pressure generated is correlated with the flow rate, allowing for accurate measurement of fluid velocity in various applications. These devices are commonly employed in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, water treatment, and HVAC systems.</p><p>The Orifice Flow Meter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.3% during the forecast period.
