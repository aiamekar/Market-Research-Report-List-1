<p><p>The Audible Outdoor Warning System market primarily consists of three types: omnidirectional, rotating, and directional systems. Omnidirectional systems emit sound in all directions, ensuring wide coverage for alerting large areas. Rotating systems feature a rotating horn that projects sound in sweeping patterns, enhancing coverage while reducing the number of units needed. Directional systems focus sound in a specific direction, allowing for targeted alerts, which can be effective in urban environments where sound needs to be directed toward specific populations.</p></p>
