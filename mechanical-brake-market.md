<p><p>The mechanical brake market encompasses various types, including electromagnetic brakes and mechanical hydraulic brakes. Electromagnetic brakes utilize electromagnetic force to engage and disengage the braking system, offering precise control and rapid response, making them ideal for applications like elevators and conveyor systems. In contrast, mechanical hydraulic brakes rely on hydraulic fluid pressure to create braking force, providing strong stopping power and durability, suited for automotive and heavy machinery markets. Both types are critical for safety and performance in various industries.</p></p>
