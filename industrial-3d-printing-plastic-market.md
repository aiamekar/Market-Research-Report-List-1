<p><strong>The Industrial 3D Printing Plastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Filament</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial 3D Printing Plastic Market consists of three primary types: powder, filament, and liquid. Powder-based printing utilizes fine plastic particles for techniques like Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), allowing for complex geometries and strong prints. Filament is used in Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), where thermoplastic strands are melted and extruded layer by layer, ideal for prototyping. Liquid printing employs resin, typically in Stereolithography (SLA), curing polymers using light to create highly detailed and smooth-surface objects. Each type supports diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
