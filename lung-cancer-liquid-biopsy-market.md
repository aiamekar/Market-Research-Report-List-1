<p><strong>The Lung Cancer Liquid Biopsy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exosomes and RNA</li><li>CTCs and ctDNA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lung cancer liquid biopsy market encompasses three primary types: exosomes and RNA, circulating tumor cells (CTCs), and circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA). Exosomes and RNA involve biomarker analysis from extracellular vesicles that facilitate tumor-cell communication. CTCs are intact cancer cells found in the bloodstream, providing insights into tumor characteristics. ctDNA refers to fragmented tumor-derived DNA circulating in the blood, aiding in genetic mutation profiling. Together, these modalities enhance early diagnosis, monitoring, and individualized treatment strategies for lung cancer.</p></p>
